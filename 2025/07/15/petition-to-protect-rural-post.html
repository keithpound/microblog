Petition to Protect Rural Post Offices

Jul 15, 2025

Our post office and the one in the neighbouring village have closed in recent months, which harms our communities, especially the older residents and those without transport.

I received the attached letter from my member of parliament today, who has started a petition calling on the government to support rural post offices.

It’s not just for folks in Devon, but all UK rural areas. Please sign if you are able.
